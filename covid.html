Our COVID Protocols

We have reopened our reception room and front desk with limited capacity. Please be prompt for your appointment. With our efforts to maximize social distancing and our commitment to the highest standards of infection control, we may not be able to accommodate you if you are late. We ask that patients only be accompanied by one escort if needed.

Masks

We ask that everyone entering the office please wear a mask at all times except for when they are being treated. In addition to masks, our staff will be wearing increased PPE and because of this the temperature in the office may be a bit cooler than normal.

Hand Sanitizer

Please do not wear gloves as you will be asked to sanitize your hands before entering the office.

Temperature Check

A temperature screening is mandatory for all patients prior to removal of your mask for treatment. If your temperature is 100.4 °F or higher, or you are exhibiting flu-like symptoms, we will have to reschedule your appointment for at least 14 days in the future.

Contact Tracing

If you develop any signs/symptoms or are diagnosed with COVID-19 within the next 14 days please contact our office immediately.
